For Medical Professionals

The quality and dedication of our more than 4,000 doctors, nurses, and allied health professionals make Cedars-Sinai one of the world's leading academic medical centers. As a medical professional, you'll have the opportunity to collaborate with specialists across a wide range of clinical areas and benefit from our longstanding commitment to innovation and education.

Become an Affiliate or Join the Medical Staff

If you would like to affiliate with Cedars-Sinai, you may contact the MediCare Medical Network for more information about participating in one of our affiliated IPAs or medical groups.

To apply to the Medical Staff, please call Medical Staff Services for application instructions.
