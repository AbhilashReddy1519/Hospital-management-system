Apply Now: PhD in Health Artificial Intelligence

The PhD in Health Artificial Intelligence Program (AI) offers rigorous training in AI algorithms and methods, with a focus on analyzing clinical data to enhance patient care. Our hands-on, active approach to teaching reinforces AI concepts through clinical rotations and scholarly collaboration with physicians and medical staff.

Graduates will be positioned to directly improve healthcare and patient outcomes through the rigorous development and deployment of AI algorithms and software. MediCare PhD in Health AI is pending WSCUC accreditation.
